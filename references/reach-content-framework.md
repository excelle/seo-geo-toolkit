# REACH Content Quality Framework

REACH scores content across five dimensions before publication. **SEO Score = average of R, E, A, C dimensions. GEO Score = average of H dimension signals.**

## Dimensions

| Dimension | Letter | Items | Focus |
|-----------|--------|-------|-------|
| Relevance | R | 14 | Keyword harmony, intent match, topical depth |
| Engagement | E | 16 | Readability, hook quality, interaction design |
| Authority | A | 18 | Evidence, citations, expertise signals |
| Clarity | C | 16 | Structure, formatting, cognitive load |
| Health | H | 8 | Freshness, accuracy, safety, accessibility |

## Scoring

- Each item scored 0, 1, or 2
- Sum per dimension, convert to percentage
- Overall REACH Score = average of all five dimensions
- Veto items override the score: V02, V11, V21

## Veto Items

| ID | Dimension | Item | Failure Consequence |
|----|-----------|------|---------------------|
| V02 | Relevance | Keyword stuffing or hidden text | BLOCK publication |
| V11 | Authority | Factual inaccuracy or fabricated source | BLOCK publication |
| V21 | Health | Harmful, hateful, or dangerous content | BLOCK publication |

## Thresholds

| REACH Score | Verdict |
|-------------|---------|
| 0-49 | Needs major revision |
| 50-69 | Needs refinement |
| 70-84 | Acceptable with notes |
| 85-100 | Clear to publish |

## Dimension Breakdown

### Relevance (R) — 14 items
1. R01: Primary keyword in H1
2. R02: Primary keyword in first 100 words
3. R03: Secondary keywords distributed naturally
4. R04: Search intent matched (info/commercial/transactional)
5. R05: Content depth matches top-ranking competitors
6. R06: Topical clusters covered (pillar + spokes)
7. R07: Related entities and synonyms present
8. R08: GEO-relevant questions answered
9. R09: Title front-loads primary keyword
10. R10: Meta description includes keyword and CTA
11. R11: URL slug contains keyword
12. R12: Image alt text optimized
13. R13: Internal links use descriptive anchors
14. R14: No keyword cannibalization with existing pages

### Engagement (E) — 16 items
1. E01: Hook in first sentence captures attention
2. E02: Scannable structure with clear H2/H3 hierarchy
3. E03: Bullet lists and tables used for complex data
4. E04: Average paragraph ≤ 4 sentences
5. E05: Active voice dominant (>70%)
6. E06: Power words and emotional triggers present
7. E07: Storytelling or case study element included
8. E08: Questions interspersed to engage reader
9. E09: Comparison or contrast table present where relevant
10. E10: Statistic or data point every 200-300 words
11. E11: Visual placeholder or diagram suggestion included
12. E12: Progress indicators for long content (TOC or summary)
13. E13: Strong CTA with clear next step
14. E14: Social proof or testimonial referenced
15. E15: Content length matches SERP-winning average
16. E16: Mobile readability verified (short lines, tap targets)

### Authority (A) — 18 items
1. A01: Author byline with credentials
2. A02: Publication date and last-updated date visible
3. A03: External citations to authoritative domains
4. A04: Statistics backed by named sources
5. A05: Expert quotes or interviews included
6. A06: Original research or analysis present
7. A07: Case study with methodology disclosed
8. A08: Industry-standard terminology used correctly
9. A09: Counter-arguments or limitations acknowledged
10. A10: References section or inline citations
11. A11: Domain matches topic (relevant niche authority)
12. A12: About page linked for E-E-A-T
13. A13: Reviewed or fact-checked label present
14. A14: No unverifiable claims
15. A15: Data presented with context (not cherry-picked)
16. A16: Third-party endorsements or awards mentioned
17. A17: Correct schema markup for authorship
18. A18: Linked to from authoritative pages on site

### Clarity (C) — 16 items
1. C01: H1 present and unique
2. C02: Logical heading hierarchy (no skipped levels)
3. C03: Table of contents for content >1000 words
4. C04: Conclusion summarizes key takeaways
5. C05: FAQ section for question-keywords
6. C06: Definitions provided for jargon
7. C07: Consistent terminology throughout
8. C08: Code blocks properly formatted if technical
9. C09: Math or formulas explained step by step
10. C10: Breadcrumb or navigation context clear
11. C11: Page purpose obvious within 3 seconds
12. C12: No intrusive interstitials blocking content
13. C13: Font size and contrast accessible
14. C14: Link destinations clear from anchor text
15. C15: No broken internal or external links
16. C16: Search snippet preview optimized

### Health (H) — 8 items
1. H01: Content published or updated within last 12 months
2. H02: All claims fact-checked against current sources
3. H03: No broken external references
4. H04: No misleading or clickbait framing
5. H05: Accessible to screen readers (alt text, ARIA)
6. H06: No hate speech, discrimination, or harassment
7. H07: No dangerous instructions without warnings
8. H08: Privacy and compliance notices present if required
